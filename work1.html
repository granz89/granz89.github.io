Wish List - App Design

The Gifter is an app that allows the user to scan and record potential gift ideas for themselves or others. It is designed for people who never know what to get the important people in their life and those who find difficulty in providing other people with ideas for themselves. It is simple, as it will have the user scan a barcode of the potential gift in store, it will than store the item in a designated list and will note the cost, location and date the item was found. The user can assign lists with different friend names and can return to see stored information at a later date. The app will also provide location information on where to buy and cost. This app is an easy place to store all necessary product info without the hassle of writing it down or relying on memory.
