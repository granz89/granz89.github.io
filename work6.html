Mud Hero- Logo Redesign Design

Mud Hero is a Canadian run obstacle course that travels throughout Canada raising money for local charities. This project focused on redesigning their current company brand. The concept behind this redesign was to integrate the hero aspect and to develop it into a superhero emblem design. It fused the race and hero feeling by incorporating a tire, a common part of obstacle races, into the emblem.
